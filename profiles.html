Maii of Nochushima

Native Fruit: Cherry
Dream Address: DA-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX
Favourite Villager: Sherb

My name is Maii and I've been playing ACNH since launch! I've been an avid Animal Crossing fan and player since the Gamecube version and have logged probably close to 6000 hours across all games! I love being able to design my villagers' houses as well my own and now, even personalise an entire island! Though the terraforming mechanic in the game isn't the best, it's has given me great freedom to unleash my creativity without any consequences and I really enjoy that aspect about this series.
